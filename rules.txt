seedsGame Rules:

Player has to find pairs of numbers that are equal or sum up to 10

Example:
Valid pairs: (1, 1), (2, 8), (6, 4)
Invalid pairs: (1, 4), (2, 6)

There must be no numbers between pair members

Console version of the game lets you point on the pair by typing in coordinates of each number. Row number goes first, column number goes second.try:
    
As player runs out of available pairs on the grid, new numbers row is added automatically.

Have a nice play!
